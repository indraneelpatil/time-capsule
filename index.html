MRSD Time Capsule

Drift bottles were used in the late 19th century to study ocean currents. They carried messages from the sender and followed the currents until they reached a receiver, in some cases in another century, just like a time capsule.

Use this page to create and send a drift bottle with your imagination of robotics in 2070 (only restricted by the laws of physics). Copy of all drift bottles will be sent to you by email in 2070 and will always be visible on this page until then.
