MRSD Time Capsule

Drift bottles have been used in the late 19th century to study ocean currents and even by doomed world war 1 soldiers to send their last messages. They carried messages from the sender and followed the currents until they reached a receiver, in some cases in another century, just like a time capsule.

Use this page to create and send a drift bottle with your imagination of robotics in 2060 (only restricted by the laws of physics). Copy of all drift bottles will be sent to you by email in 2060 and will always be visible on this page until then. So that you can observe everyones' predictions age like fine wine or like bananas.
